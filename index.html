How to buy

1

Create a Wallet

Download metamask or your wallet of choice from the app store or google play store for free. Desktop users, download the google chrome extension by going to metamask.io.
2

Get Some ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to switch to $USDT. If you don't have any ETH, you can buy directly on metamask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.
3

Go to Uniswap

Connect to Uniswap. Go to app.uniswap.org in google chrome or on the browser inside your Meatamask app. Connect your wallet. Paste the $USDT token address into Uniswap, select MARIO, and confirm. When Metamask prompts you for a wallet signature, sign.
4

Switch ETH for $USDT

Switch ETH for $USDT. We recommend to use 5% slippage during times of market volatility.
